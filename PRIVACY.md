# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-11_

**Base64 Converter** is designed with privacy as a first principle.

## The Short Version

- **Everything runs in your browser.** Encoding, decoding, and file conversion
  are performed entirely on your device using client-side JavaScript.
- **We do not collect, store, or transmit your data.** The text you type and the
  files you load **never leave your computer** and are never uploaded to any
  server.
- **No accounts, no tracking cookies.** The application does not require sign-in
  and does not set advertising or analytics cookies.

## Data You Provide

Any text or file you convert is processed in memory in your browser and
discarded when you close or refresh the page. It is not persisted anywhere by
this application beyond the current session.

## Local Storage

The application uses your browser's `localStorage` only to remember your
**preferences**, such as:

- Your **dark / light mode** choice.
- Your selected **interface language**.

This information stays in your browser, is never transmitted, and can be cleared
at any time via your browser settings.

## Hosting

The live version is served as static files via **GitHub Pages**. As with any web
host, GitHub may collect standard technical logs (such as IP addresses) subject
to [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
This project itself does not control or receive that data.

## Third-Party Links

The app links to external sites (for example, the maintainer's website and
"buy me a coffee" page). Those sites have their own privacy policies, and this
policy does not apply to them.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
